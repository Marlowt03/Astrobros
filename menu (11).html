Our Menu

Stacker

Build your own layered sundae starting with our silky soft‑serve and adding your choice of cereals, cookies, syrups and candy. Each layer is piled high to create a towering delight.

Dubai Chocolate

Indulge in our most decadent chocolate offering inspired by the luxurious tastes of Dubai. We blend premium cocoa with hints of cardamom and top it with gold‑dust sprinkles.

Taiyaki

Our take on the popular Japanese street snack. Choose from fillings like Nutella, custard or red bean, then top the warm fish‑shaped waffle with your favourite soft‑serve swirl.

Michigan Pothole

A tribute to the famous flavor featuring dark chocolate ice cream riddled with chocolate chunks and thick fudge, just like the potholes in our northern neighbour’s roads.

Seasonal Flavours

We rotate a variety of limited‑time flavors through the year. Check back often to see what’s new: from Strawberries & Cream to Apple Pie a la Mode and Birthday Cake. There’s always something special happening at AstroBros!
