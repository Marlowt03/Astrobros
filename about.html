About AstroBros

Fueled by passion and a love for ice cream, AstroBros went from a wild dream to the place Parma’s kids and families can’t stop talking about.

Our Story

Along the way, AstroBros has become more than a shop—it’s a gathering place where the community shows up. Kids stop in after practice and light up when they see themselves featured on our socials, teammates crowd the counter to celebrate a win (or shake off a loss), and families make it their go-to spot for a treat that feels a little bigger than dessert. What started with one guy chasing a dream has grown into a place where people feel like part of the story.

That spirit is what keeps AstroBros moving forward. Every scoop isn’t just about ice cream—it’s about connection, energy, and giving Parma something to rally around. Whether it’s a signature stacker piled high or a simple cone shared with friends, AstroBros has become the backdrop for everyday moments that turn into lasting memories.
