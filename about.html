About AstroBros

We are two brothers with a love for ice cream and coffee. What started as a dream has become Parma’s favorite place for out‑of‑this‑world desserts.

Our Story

Founded with a mission to bring cosmic joy to every scoop, AstroBros Ice Cream & Coffee blends traditional techniques with creative flavors. Our shop combines a relaxed, welcoming atmosphere with handcrafted treats that invite you to slow down and savor the moment.

Whether it’s our signature stackers layered with toppings, or classic scoops made with premium ingredients, we pour our heart into everything we create. Thank you for being part of our journey!
